About me

Hi, my name is Ben Cole, I am a young, budding Web Designer/Developer and Social Media designer. Cole webworks is a passion project, with a philosophy to encourage growth of digital media in the South West. I currently have lived in Cornwall all my life, and have loved every minute of it.


History

I attended Redruth School for five years, and the Sixth Form for a year, before leaving after finding it didn't suit what I wanted to do for the future. I've been looking since then, for a route into the Technology sector, and have completed a lot of work experience as well as self-teaching myself a few programming languages.


Goals and Aspirations

My goals are to break into the digital sector within the next year, whether through an apprenticeship or a junior role, and grow my skills alongside those who have experience, as my own learning can only go so far. I seek to prove my methods to people and allow myself to grow.


Interests

My interests are hugely based through technology, whether it be the newest gadgets or gizmos, tinkering with them to see what I can do with them. I also love working on/with websites and designing branding for people's businesses, as I can get to know them, and their passions better also. Other than this, I enjoy reading and listening to audio books, my favourite trilogy being Timothy Zahn's Star Wars novels based around the character, Grand Admiral Thrawn.


Hobbies

My hobbies consists of a love of Dungeons and Dragons, taking walks through the South West and swimming at my local leisure centre. Aside from this, I enjoy scouring Cornwall, and visit places I've not yet been to before; watching YouTube for interesting videos and creators, and watching content on Twitch.tv.
